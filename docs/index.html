Public standards against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Developer: Samuil Nedelchev  ·  Last updated: 2026-03-13

Our commitment

TheFunCommunity is a community app designed to bring people together to chat and have fun — including sharing and enjoying music culture such as old-school record players. We are committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users.

We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior involving child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Prohibited content and behavior

The following is strictly prohibited on TheFunCommunity:

  • Any content that depicts, promotes, normalizes, or facilitates CSAE/CSAM.
  • Grooming, sexual solicitation of minors, or attempts to arrange exploitative contact.
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing sexual content involving minors, or links to such content.
  • Any exploitation, coercion, extortion, or trafficking involving minors.
  • Any attempt to evade detection, moderation, or reporting processes.

Reporting and user feedback

If you see content or behavior that may violate these standards, please report it immediately:

  • In-app: use the app’s feedback/reporting feature (for example, Help → Report / Send feedback), if available in your version.
  • Email: snedelchev@gmail.com
  • Support page: N/A

Tip: include screenshots, usernames, message links, and dates to help us act quickly.

Enforcement

We may take the following actions (including without notice) when we detect or receive reports of violations:

  • Remove content and restrict features.
  • Suspend or permanently ban accounts involved in CSAE-related activity.
  • Preserve relevant information as required for safety, security, and legal compliance.

CSAM handling and legal compliance

When we become aware of confirmed CSAM or CSAE-related activity, we will take appropriate action consistent with applicable child safety laws and may cooperate with relevant authorities.
